Demo Plan

May 19, 2016

Space requirements

20 x 20 ft.

Furniture, props

1 lab desk, 4 chairs

Compute, networking, power requirements, number of hololenses needed

1 lab desktop, wireless network, 3 Hololens Devices, 1 power strip

Any equipment you need to buy for the demo

None

Your demo experience

We plan on having a persistent hololens session for all participants (clear if it gets too cluttered) that starts with a pre-existing stage consisting of multiple holograms (most likely a graffiti wall and some fish). Each demo session will have at least 2 participants (team members fill in if not enough people) in order to showcase the collaborative experience of our application.

At the start, participants will be able to choose from a selection of holograms, including a graffiti wall, car, trees, etc. and be able to place them in the session.

During and after selection, we will guide each participant through the placement and painting modes to familiarize them with the application. This includes things like how to draw, how to manipulate holograms, how to change the brush color and size, and how to erase/clear the drawings

From then on, we will allow them to freely draw and place new holograms.
